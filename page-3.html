HOW I SEE ME

WELL, HOW DO I SEE ME?

Sometimes I think of myself as someone everyone loves to hate. In reality, I find people don't care enough to hate me. I have truly let the opinions of others create a negative perception of myself.

When I think of myself kindly, I think of myself as a thoughtful friend. I think of myself as someone my sister looks up to. I think of myself as a creative, someone who has explored many mediums and enjoyed them all.

I HAVE LEARNED TO LOVE THIS POSITIVE PERCEPTION OF THE SELF, DESPITE THE LINGERING NEGATIVE PERCEPTION.
